The Beginning

A Lifelong Love of Light

Alexander Reed discovered photography at age twelve with a borrowed film camera. What started as curiosity grew into an obsession — the way light dances across a face, the fraction of a second that separates an ordinary image from an extraordinary one.

After studying Fine Art Photography at the Rhode Island School of Design, Alexander spent years assisting world-renowned photographers in New York and Paris before founding Aperture & Light in 2013.

Today, the studio is celebrated for its distinctive editorial style — clean, luminous, and deeply human. Every session is approached not as a job, but as an opportunity to reveal something true.

Philosophy

What We Believe

✦

Light Is Everything

We are students of light — natural, shaped, and found. It's the single most important element in every image we create.

◈

Emotion Over Perfection

A technically perfect image means nothing without feeling. We chase the real, the raw, the moments between the poses.

△

Less Is More

Our aesthetic is intentionally minimal. Clean compositions, muted tones, and generous negative space let the subject breathe.
